Overview

My work bridges quantitative analysis and practical engineering: large‑scale tactile neural simulations (TouchSim), spectral methods (PSD, coherence), and reproducible pipelines on HPC. Prior projects include biomedical device prototyping (vacuum systems, test instrumentation) and an applied analytics toolkit in Python. I enjoy turning technical analysis into decisions, clear visuals, and testable hypotheses.
